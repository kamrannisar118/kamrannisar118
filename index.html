Muhammad Kamran Nisar (Cheena)

Muhammad Kamran Nisar aka Cheena was born in 2005, in the suburbs of Lahore, Pakistan, in the town of Sharaqpur. He belongs to the "Cheena" faction of the Jatt tribe of the Punjab. His hometown is famous for its special dish: the gulab-jamuns. And so the resemblance can be found between his traits and the dish: the sweeter, the blacker.

Like any other individual, his life started as an ordinary soul, but he had an ambition and burning desire to achieve his ambition. This will also be discussed here but let's start from the very beginning of the story.

The Etymology of Cheena

Cheena, bascically, is the name of a cast, derived from the Jatt clan of Punjab. As a historical background, it is a faction of the Jatt tribe of Punjab that migrated in a large number to Pakistani territory after the partition of 1947.
